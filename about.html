About Me

I am a recent graduate of Temple University who now works as a Software Engineer for Tata Consultancy Services. While at Temple, I receivced a Bachelors Degree in Information Science and Technology.

The biggest project I undertook during my time at Temple would be my senior Capstone which was a two-semester project in which I and a team of 4 other Temple IS&T majors created a change management system for Temple's Student Collaboration Center. This involved the creation of different client deliverables and client meetings to deliver a web service that allows users and admins to submit and modify change requests. Using ASP.NET and other services to create the webpage and database that will provide the service.
